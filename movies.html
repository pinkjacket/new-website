Movies page

Here is a list of movies that I like

  • Snowpiercer

    After Earth freezes over, the survivors of humanity take refuge on an unstoppable train.

  • Skyfall

    In one of the best-received films in the series, Daniel Craig's Bond confronts his past.

  • Nightcrawler

    A reporter goes to horrifying lengths to get stories.

  • Adaptation

    Nicolas Cage plays a fictional version of film scribe Charlie Kaufman, suffering a breakdown while attempting to adapt The Orchid Thief.

  • Fight club

    Disaffected men form a fight club, and try to overthrow the government.

  • Shining

    A new caretaker and his family are tormented by a haunted hotel.

  • La vita e bella

    A man tries to shield his son from the realities of life under fascism.

  • Free Fire

    In 1978, a gun sale in an abandoned warehouse goes awry and everyone starts shooting.

  • Gangs of New York

    A highly fictionalized but entertaining account of 1800s New York, based on the book by Herbert Asbury.
